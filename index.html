ASRJC ICT Champions

The ASRJC ICT Champions programme is an initiative at Anderson Serangoon Junior College in Singapore, aimed at enhancing digital literacy among students. The programme provides students with opportunities to develop and apply their ICT skills through various activities and projects. ICT Champions act as peer leaders, guiding their schoolmates and participating in events that promote digital literacy skills across the college community.
